Gia to prwto meros,sto TCPClient diavazei to string apo ton xristh kai meta to stelnoyme ston Server.
Epeita o TCPServer lamvanei to string kai gia na diaxoriso to string koitao ena ena xaraktira toy string 
mexri na vro '$', opou kseroyme oti einai to telos toy string. Se kathe xaraktira pou koitao vlepo ama einai '\'
kai an to epomeno apo auto einai 'n' tote se ena Arraylist apo string apothikevo oti koitoysa tosi ora to opoio den
itan '\'. Molis ftaso sto telos toy string kano ena rand sta stoixia tou Arraylist kai to stelno piso ston Client.

Gia to deutero meros, ekana ena kainourgio client me to onoma STCPClient. Ayto pou kanei einai na pairnei ena String
apo ton user kai na kanei split gia na paro to method. An to method einai GET tote apla stelno to string allios ama
einai PUT tote kanei tis aparaitites erotiseis kai afou ftiaksei string to stelnei ston webserver. O Webserver se
mia while trexei oste na kanei diadoxika aitimata. Afou parei to string apo ton client pairnei to proto token kai 
vlepei ama einai valid. An to method einai GET tote koitao ama yparxei to arxeio kai epistrefei ta dedomena tou
kai ftiaxnei kai ton katalilo header. Allios an einai PUT kai den yparxei to arxeio to opoio zitaei o client na
ftiaksei tote pairnei to body, to opoio theoro ego oti teleionei me </p>, tha mporoyse na teleionei me opiodipote
allo xaraktira arkei na to kserei o client. Telos grafei mesa sto arxeio to body kai epistrefei ston client to
katalilo minima.
